Lasagne!

Recipe for a rich and tasty lasagne

Delight your taste buds with this quick and easy recipe!

Anyone can make it! If you prefer the vegetarian type, we got you covered!

Ingredients

  • 500 gr ground pork/beef mix
  • lasagne noodles (if fresh and with eggs, better)
  • 1 Mutti sauce
  • 1-2 carrots

    • For the béchamel

  • milk
  • flour
  • nutmeg
  • butter
    •

    Steps

    1. Chop the carrots and fry them until softened
    2. Add the beef and let brown. If there is a lot of water, let it evaporate
    3. Season with salt, pepper, cumin and red pepper
    4. Add tomato sauce, reduce heat and let simmer.
    5. Make the béchamel by melting the butter on low heat, adding the flour
    6. Let it become a golden paste, add tiny bit of nutmeg
    7. While continuously whisking add milk slowly
    8. Build the dish by alternating white and red sauce, top with white sauce
    9. Grate parmesan before taking out
