Veggie Lasagne!

Recipe for a rich and tasty veggie lasagne

Delight your taste buds with this quick and easy recipe!

Anyone can make it! If you prefer the meat type, we got you covered!

Ingredients

  • frozen spinach
  • tomato sauce
  • lasagne noodles (if fresh and with eggs -> better!)
  • cream
  • mozarella

Steps

  1. Cook the spinach
  2. Add the tomato sauce and cheeses of your choice
  3. Add the cream
  4. Assemble
  5. Enjoy!
